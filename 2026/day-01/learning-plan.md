# What is DevOps ?
DevOps is a practice followed in IT domain to bring development & operations team together so that they can collaborate, build a software & deliever quicker to users. It brings high availability & reliability in the SDLC process. With the use of Continous Integration (CI) / Continous Delivery (CD), the process is automated which makes the SDLC quicker. It also provides scaling & healing capability to meet the on-demand. Observability is also one of the key aspects it covers which helps to keep applications highly available at any point of time for the users.
